Terzanelle

Description

A nineteen-line poem divided into six stanzas: five interlocking triplets and a quatrain. Of French and Italian origin, it adapts terza rima to the villanelle form by the use of a repeton: A1 B A2, bCB, cDC, dED, eFE, f A1 (or F) F (or A 1) A2. (A1 and A2 are refrains; BCDEF are repetitions.)
