Quatrain

Description

A stanza of four lines, the most common form in English poetry. Quatrains consist of four lines of verse, rhymed or unrhymed: the envelope quatrain rhyme runs abba; the couplet quatrain runs aabb; the alternating quatrain runs abab; and the monorhyme quatrain runs aaaa.
